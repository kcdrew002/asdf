Why Exercise Matters

You may know that exercise is good for you, but just how good can it really be? One of the more popular reasons people exercise is to control their weight and burn calories. But exercise has far more benefits, a few include helping to reduce anxiety, improving your mood, boosting your energy, supporting better sleep, reducing your chances of having a stroke, type 2 diabetes, and many forms of cancer.

Exploring Your City

St. Louis has a wide variety of trails for walking, running, and biking. Below are a few places you can go to reach your fitness goals in your city. 

Kiener Plaza

Downtown St. Louis has a variety of attractions in close proximity, and Kiener Plaza is a great place to start your city exploration.

Castlewood State Park

This state park offers a variety of outdoor activities and an escape from your everyday city envoirnment. If you enjoy hiking, biking, or horse back riding, consider spending your free time here.

Laumeier Scupture Garden

If you're a fan of the Arts, this scupture garden is a great place to walk around while viewing various works of art. This park in particular features over 60 scuptures throughout its 1.4 mile walking trial.
